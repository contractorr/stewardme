## 1. Construction Fundamentals

### Core Concept: Building Physical Infrastructure
Construction = process of creating buildings and infrastructure. Project-based, fragmented, low-margin industry with complex coordination requirements.

**The challenge:** Unique projects, many stakeholders, physical site constraints, weather, labor variability

### Major Sectors

| Sector | Description | Key characteristics |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Residential** | Single/multi-family homes | Volume-based, consumer-driven |
| **Commercial** | Office, retail, hospitality | Longer timelines, complex |
| **Industrial** | Factories, warehouses | Specialized requirements |
| **Infrastructure** | Roads, bridges, utilities | Government funded, long cycles |
| **Heavy civil** | Dams, tunnels, airports | Mega-projects, specialized |
| **Specialty** | Electrical, plumbing, HVAC | Trade-specific work |

## 3. Business Model Deep Dive

### GC Economics

**Revenue recognition:** % completion method
```
Contract value × % complete = Revenue recognized
```

**Margin structure:**
- Gross margin: 8-15% typical
- Net margin: 2-5% typical
- High revenue, low margin business

**Contract types:**

| Type | Description | Risk |
|------|-------------|------|
| **Lump sum/Fixed price** | Fixed total price | Contractor bears cost overrun risk |
| **Cost-plus** | Reimbursed costs + fee | Owner bears cost risk |
| **GMP** | Guaranteed Maximum Price | Shared risk |
| **T&M** | Time and materials | Owner bears risk |

**Key metrics:**
- Backlog (contracted work not yet completed)
- Book-to-bill ratio
- Project margin
- Change order rate
- Safety (TRIR - Total Recordable Incident Rate)

### Subcontractor Economics

- Bid on trade-specific scope
- Margin: 5-15% depending on specialty
- Working capital challenges (pay-when-paid clauses)
- Retention holdback (5-10% until completion)

### Home Builder Economics

**Public builders:** D.R. Horton, Lennar, PulteGroup, NVR, Toll Brothers

**Model:**
```
Home price - Land cost - Hard costs - Soft costs - SG&A = Profit
```

**Key metrics:**
- Closings (units sold)
- Average selling price (ASP)
- Gross margin: 20-25%
- Net margin: 10-15%
- Lot supply (years of owned/controlled lots)
- Cancellation rate

### Project Budget Breakdown (typical commercial)

| Category | % of total |
|----------|------------|
| Hard costs (labor + materials) | 70-75% |
| Soft costs (design, permits, fees) | 15-20% |
| Contingency | 5-10% |
| Financing costs | 3-5% |

## 7. Market Dynamics

### Market Size

- US construction spending: ~$2T annually
- Residential: ~$800B
- Commercial: ~$500B
- Infrastructure: ~$400B
- Industrial: ~$300B

### Industry Structure

**Highly fragmented:**
- 700K+ construction firms in US
- Top 400 contractors: <20% market share
- Average firm: <10 employees
- Regional markets matter

**Labor:**
- 8M+ construction workers in US
- Shortage of skilled labor
- Aging workforce
- High turnover

### Key Challenges

**Productivity:**
- Construction productivity flat vs other industries
- Fragmentation limits tech adoption
- Project complexity increasing

**Labor:**
- Skilled worker shortage
- Immigration restrictions
- Training gaps
- Safety

**Materials:**
- Price volatility
- Supply chain disruptions
- Lead time variability

**Risk:**
- Thin margins, high risk
- Payment issues (especially for subs)
- Litigation common

### Regulatory Environment

- Building codes (IBC, local amendments)
- OSHA safety requirements
- Permitting (varies by jurisdiction)
- Environmental regulations
- Prevailing wage (government projects)
- Licensing requirements (varies by trade/state)

## Quick Reference: Project Flow

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                 PRE-CONSTRUCTION                             │
│  Planning → Design → Permitting → Bidding → Contracts       │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                              ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                   CONSTRUCTION                               │
│  Mobilize → Sitework → Structure → MEP → Finishes → Punch   │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                              ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    CLOSEOUT                                  │
│  Substantial completion → Punchlist → Final → Warranty      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Payment flow:**
```
Owner → GC → Subcontractors → Suppliers
         ↑        ↑              ↑
    Retainage   Retainage    Pay-when-paid
```
