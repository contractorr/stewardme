## 1. Real Estate Fundamentals

### Core Concept: Land, Buildings, and Space
Real estate = land and permanent structures. Combines physical asset ownership with space-as-a-service. Second-largest asset class globally.

**Value drivers:** Location, supply constraints, cash flow (rent), appreciation, tax benefits

### Major Sectors

| Sector | Description | Key characteristics |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Residential** | Single-family, multifamily, condos | Consumer demand driven, local markets |
| **Commercial office** | Office buildings, co-working | Tenant quality matters, long leases |
| **Retail** | Shopping centers, malls, street retail | E-commerce disruption, foot traffic |
| **Industrial** | Warehouses, logistics, data centers | E-commerce tailwind, long-term leases |
| **Hospitality** | Hotels, resorts | RevPAR driven, operational intensity |
| **Specialty** | Healthcare, self-storage, senior living | Demographic-driven, operational |

## 3. Business Model Deep Dive

### Development Economics

**Pro forma basics:**
```
Gross Potential Revenue (GPV)
- Vacancy & credit loss
= Effective Gross Income (EGI)
- Operating expenses
= Net Operating Income (NOI)
```

**Return metrics:**
```
Cap rate = NOI / Property value
Cash-on-cash = Annual cash flow / Equity invested
IRR = Time-weighted return including exit
Equity multiple = Total distributions / Equity invested
```

**Development math:**
```
Development cost = Land + Hard costs + Soft costs + Financing
Yield on cost = Stabilized NOI / Total development cost
Spread to market cap rate = Development profit
```

### Residential Brokerage Economics

**Commission structure:**
- Typical: 5-6% of sale price (split buyer/seller agent)
- Agents get 60-90% of their side (split with brokerage)
- Brokerage bears costs: leads, tech, compliance, office

**Agent economics:**
- Most agents: <$50K income
- Top agents: $500K+
- Median home sale: ~$400K → ~$12K gross commission per side

### Property Management Economics

**Residential:**
- Management fee: 8-12% of collected rent
- Leasing fee: 50-100% of first month rent
- Ancillary: maintenance markup, fees

**Commercial:**
- Management fee: 2-5% of collected rent
- Leasing commissions: 4-6% of total lease value
- Construction management fees

### REIT Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Typical range |
|--------|------------|---------------|
| **FFO** | Funds From Operations (earnings + depreciation) | Core metric |
| **AFFO** | Adjusted FFO (capex deducted) | More conservative |
| **NAV** | Net Asset Value | Premium/discount to stock |
| **Occupancy** | % of space leased | 90%+ target |
| **NOI margin** | NOI / Revenue | 60-70% |
| **Debt/EBITDA** | Leverage metric | 5-7x typical |

## 7. Market Dynamics

### Market Size
- US residential real estate: ~$45T value
- US commercial real estate: ~$20T value
- Annual home sales: ~5-6M transactions, ~$2T volume
- Residential brokerage revenue: ~$100B

### Cycle Dynamics

**Residential:**
- Interest rate sensitive (mortgage rates)
- Demographics (household formation)
- Supply constraints (zoning, construction costs)
- 7-10 year cycles typically

**Commercial:**
- Capital markets driven
- Sector-specific cycles
- Cap rate compression/expansion
- Development pipeline timing

### Current Market Themes (2024-25)
- Higher rates → transaction volume down
- Office uncertainty (WFH impact)
- Industrial still strong (e-commerce)
- Multifamily oversupply in some markets
- Housing affordability crisis

### Regulatory Environment

**Residential:**
- Fair Housing Act
- RESPA (Real Estate Settlement Procedures)
- State licensing requirements
- NAR/MLS rule changes (commission lawsuits)

**Commercial:**
- Zoning and entitlements
- Environmental (Phase I/II)
- ADA compliance
- 1031 exchanges (tax deferral)

**PropTech specific:**
- Fair housing in algorithms
- Data privacy
- Licensing (brokerage, lending)

## Quick Reference: Deal Structure

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    CAPITAL STACK                             │
│  Senior Debt (60-70% LTV) → Mezz → Pref Equity → Common     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                              ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    PROPERTY                                  │
│  Land + Building → Generates NOI → Pays debt → Equity return│
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                              ↓
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    WATERFALL                                 │
│  Return of capital → Pref return → Catch-up → Promote split │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Transaction flow (residential):**
```
List → Show → Offer → Contract → Inspection → Appraisal → Clear title → Close
```
